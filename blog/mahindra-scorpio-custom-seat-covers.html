Frequently Asked Questions

Are Scorpio N seat covers different from Scorpio Classic?
Yes. The Scorpio N has different seat geometry, bolster design, and airbag configurations compared to the Scorpio Classic. WellFit maintains separate precision patterns for each model to ensure a perfect fit.
Can I get seat covers for the 7-seater Scorpio?
Absolutely. WellFit provides custom seat covers for all Scorpio seating configurations, including the 7-seater Scorpio Classic with its rear-facing jump seats and the 7-seater Scorpio N with its third-row bench.
What’s the best material for a Scorpio used for off-roading?
For off-road use, we recommend heavy-duty performance fabrics. They resist dust, mud, and moisture while remaining breathable. For a balance of luxury and practicality, PU leatherette is an excellent choice.
