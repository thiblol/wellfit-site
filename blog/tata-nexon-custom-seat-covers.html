Material Options for Tata Nexon Seat Covers

Nappa Leather — Premium Protection

Nappa leather elevates the Nexon’s cabin to true luxury territory. The soft, supple texture and rich finish complement the Nexon’s premium feel, making every drive more enjoyable. WellFit offers 12 Nappa leather colours for the Nexon, from sophisticated black and elegant beige to striking two-tone combinations.

Our Nappa leather is specially treated to resist UV damage, ensuring it stays beautiful even after years of exposure to the Indian sun. It’s the preferred choice for Nexon XZ+ and XZA+ owners who want the very best for their SUV.

PU Leatherette — Smart Value

High-quality PU leatherette offers the best value proposition for Nexon owners. It delivers genuine leather-like aesthetics at a fraction of the cost, with superior resistance to stains, spills, and cracking — making it ideal for India’s varied climate conditions.

PU leatherette is particularly popular among Nexon owners in Kerala and Hyderabad, where the combination of heat and humidity demands materials that can withstand moisture without degrading. Cleaning is as simple as a wipe with a damp cloth.

Performance Fabrics — Built for Indian Roads

For Nexon owners who prioritise breathability and comfort, performance fabrics are the ideal choice. Premium mesh and suede materials offer excellent ventilation, keeping you cool during long drives in warm weather. They also provide added grip, enhancing the driving experience on winding roads.

WellFit’s custom-fitted fabric covers match the Nexon’s seat contours perfectly, ensuring a factory-like finish that standard universal covers simply cannot achieve.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Tata Nexon seat covers airbag compatible?

Yes. All WellFit seat covers for the Tata Nexon are designed with precision side-stitching that allows airbags to deploy without obstruction. Safety is never compromised.

What seat cover material is best for the Nexon in Indian summers?

For hot Indian summers, we recommend Nappa leather or PU leatherette with UV treatment. Both materials resist heat absorption and are easy to clean. For maximum breathability, our performance fabric options are an excellent choice.

Can I install seat covers on the Nexon EV without affecting the battery warranty?

Yes. WellFit seat covers are custom-fit and require no electrical modifications. They install over the existing seats without affecting any vehicle systems, including the EV battery and electrical components.
